Today Patrick woke up early due to some mysterious sound. He's just ten years old kid who lived with his parents. Patrick went to their room. He was shocked, it was 4am and no one was there. His world is falling apart. He checked all of his rooms but he was unable to find them. He went out of his house to find them. He noticed a strange quietness in the environment. He decided to go to her neighbor Mary's house for help. He had ranged the bell many times but no one opened the door. His heartbeat was too fast. Anyone can see the terror on his face. After a few minutes, he went for the window. He had entered Mary's house. There was no life form in this house too. He came outside and screamed in front of every house on his street for help. Now, he just wanted to hear anyone's voice but no one is replying. Where was everyone and what happened to them or is this horrible Patrick's nightmare!
